## How to Choose the Right AI Tools for Your Side Hustle

Not every tool on this list is for everyone. Here's how to decide:

### Start Here (Pick 2-3 Maximum)
- **Affiliate content site:** Jasper AI + Surfer SEO + ConvertKit
- **YouTube content:** Pictory + Jasper AI + Notion
- **Email marketing focused:** ConvertKit + ConvertKit Commerce + Grammarly
- **Beginners on a budget:** Notion + Grammarly + Perplexity

### Don't Buy Everything at Once
We tested 40+ tools over 6 months. You don't need all 15. Pick 2-3 that match your current workflow, use them deeply, then add more as you scale.

### The ROI Rule
Any tool that costs money should pay for itself within 60 days. If Jasper AI at $99/mo helps you publish 10 articles that drive $300 in affiliate commissions, it's worth it. If you're not using it enough to generate ROI, cancel it.
