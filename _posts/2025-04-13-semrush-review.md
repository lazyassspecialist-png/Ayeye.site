## What We Liked

### Keyword Data Is Gold

The keyword magic tool finds opportunities you'd never discover manually:

- **Keyword difficulty** — realistic competition score (0-100)
- **SERP features** — are there featured snippets, people also ask, video results?
- **CPC** — higher CPC = more commercial intent = better for affiliate
- **Trends** — rising keywords you can capture early

We found 40 keywords with <30 difficulty and 1,000+ monthly searches that competitors weren't targeting. Those became our first 20 articles.

### Competitor Intelligence Is Instant

One competitor domain → full traffic report in seconds:
- What pages drive their most traffic
- What keywords they're ranking for
- Where their backlinks come from

Copy what's working. Find gaps they missed. Build faster than they did.

### Content Audit Saved Us 20+ Hours

Semrush's content audit feature scanned all our existing pages and told us:

- Which articles had technical SEO issues
- What internal links were broken
- Which pages needed updating

We fixed 18 issues in 3 hours. Organic traffic increased 23% the following month.

## What We Didn't Like

### The Price Is Real

$119/mo is significant. For side hustlers early in the journey, that's a real monthly cost that has to be justified by results.

Our break-even math:
- Semrush helps us rank for 5 keywords
- Those keywords drive 200 clicks/month
- 2% conversion rate = 4 sales/month
- Average affiliate commission $50 = $200/month revenue
- $200 - $119 = $81 net positive

The math works at scale. It doesn't work if you're publishing 2 articles/month.

### Overwhelming for Beginners

Semrush has 50+ tools. When you're starting, you don't need most of them.

We spent the first week lost in features. Setup takes longer than most tools.

### Some Data Is Stale

SERP data updates constantly. Semrush's database refreshes regularly but not in real-time. For fast-moving niches, this matters.

For affiliate content (typically longer keyword tail), it's fine.
