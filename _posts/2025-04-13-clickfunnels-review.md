## What We Didn't Like

### $99/mo Is Real Money

For beginners, $99/mo is a serious commitment. At that price, you need to:
- Have traffic already (or budget to drive it)
- Be serious about conversion optimization
- Have affiliate products ready to promote

It's not a "try it and see" tool. You need a plan.

### Funnels Are Not Websites

ClickFunnels pages are designed for conversion — not for SEO or discovery.

Our landing page ranked on page 2 of Google for our target keyword. That's not because ClickFunnels pages are bad for SEO — it's because funnels are built for paid traffic and email lists, not organic search.

Know the difference before you build.

### Backend Is Dated

The dashboard and editor feel older than modern SaaS tools. Not broken — just dated.

Comparing ClickFunnels' UI to ConvertKit or Thinkific feels like a generation behind.

## The Math

At $99/mo, you need to generate affiliate commissions that exceed that:

- Recommend Jasper AI ($90/sale): need 1.1 sales/month to break even
- Recommend ConvertKit ($30/sale): need 3.3 sales/month to break even
- Recommend Semrush ($200/sale): need 0.5 sales/month to break even

The high-ticket commissions (Semrush, Jasper) make this easy. Lower-ticket programs require more volume.
