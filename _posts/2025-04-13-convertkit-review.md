## What We Didn't Like

### Learning Automation Takes Time

The interface is simple but the strategy isn't. Building an effective email sequence requires understanding:

- Lead magnet strategy
- Segmenting by interest
- Timing of follow-ups
- When to sell vs. nurture

This isn't ConvertKit's fault — it's the nature of email marketing. But budget 2-3 weeks to learn properly.

### Tags vs. Segments Confusion

ConvertKit's tagging system is powerful but confusing at first:
- Tags = individual labels (subscribed from Jasper review, opened email #2)
- Segments = groups based on tags (bought product, didn't buy)

The difference matters. Take time to understand it before building automations.

### No Phone Support

Business plan ($49/mo) includes email support only. For a platform handling your income, this is a gap.
