# About ayeye

We're building real side income with AI tools — and documenting every step.

This isn't a "best AI tools" listicle site. Every tool here gets used. Every review comes from actual workflows. Every recommendation is tested before it's published.

## Why ayeye exists

In 2025, everyone is promising "AI will change your life." Most of those promises are hype. We're filtering the signal from the noise — testing tools, measuring results, and reporting back honestly.

The goal: Find the AI tools that actually help 9-to-5 workers, freelancers, and solopreneurs build meaningful side income.

## How we make money

This site uses affiliate links. When you buy something through our links, we earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. We only recommend tools we've personally tested and believe in.
